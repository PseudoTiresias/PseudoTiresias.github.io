Pseudo-Tiresias

Call me Pseudo-Tiresias, your Anonymous. Here is a collection of writings I have composed on various subjects, from politics to philosophy to personal exploration. This place serves as a space where I can speak without fear of infamy. 1 As a warning, I discuss topics of suicide, self-harm, sexual assault, misogyny, transphobia, and just a general slew of bigotries, so if you think it would be best that you avoid these, know that I enter into some detail and often personal narrative accounts of my experience regarding these within almost every single one of my essays. I write about finding meaning in spite of them.
