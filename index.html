Pseudo-Tiresias

Call me Pseudo-Tiresias, your Anonymous. Here is a collection of writings I have composed on various subjects, from politics to philosophy to personal exploration. This place serves as a space where I can speak without fear of infamy. Each essay fits in a particular cluster of essays—what we might call an "arc"—and while you don't need to read the arcs in any particular order, I would advise reading the essays within a particular arc in the order they are presented. I build on certain motifs, symbols, and themes throughout that might be lost if you pick up an arc with the third or fourth entry. 1
